About

The long
route.

Started with B.Tech Mechanical Engineering at UPES Dehradun. Graduated into closed doors and spent three years grinding banking competitive exams — which built sharper quantitative thinking than most CS grads I've met.

Worked as a Quality Analyst Engineer at RS Infra Projects using SAP S/4 HANA — got my first real taste of how data drives decisions in operations. That hooked me.

In 2024, I made a deliberate pivot — self-taught SQL, Python, Power BI, and DAX. Built a real portfolio. Then joined PG-DAC at CDAC Hyderabad to add deep CS fundamentals — Java, Spring Boot, OS, DBMS, algorithms.

I'm not a fresh graduate. I know what it means to ship under pressure, document properly, and keep going when the path isn't straight.
